DIGGING AND DEDICATION:

An unbeleivable amount of effort goes into picking your path, designing your line, creating features to guide your free fall down steep, dry terrain. Then realizing how tricky it will be to make your design come togther and getting only two people to help you pickrock, pack dust and dig for a week in the dessert. Testing stunts with high odds in the wrong way, or waiting until race day with the hopes you'll only have to send it once! Regardless, the time to enjoy the pain in your blisters can be intimidating if the most important thing is forgoten... Riding bikes is just plain old fun for any one, some just spell 'plane' differently.
